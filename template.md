# SMOOTH LEGION

## Summary of changes

Legion-specific basic metal extractors are normalized to the Armada and Cortex extractors.

The Karkinos and Decurion are cheaper, lighter, and deal slightly less damage.

Legion-specific weapons are replaced with variants available to Armada and Cortex:

- The Legion Commander uses the weapons loadout of the Armada Commander.
- Weak heatrays are red lasers. They lose their small area of effect.
- The burst-fire heatray weapon of the Telchine is replaced by a flamethrower.
- Sweepfire heatrays copy from the Beamer. They lose their small area of effect.
- Generalist railgun weapons are replaced with heavy green lasers.
- Anti-air railgun weapons are replaced with anti-air missiles. They cost less energy to fire.
- Generalist machine gun weapons are replaced with EMGs. They have higher inaccuracy and damage.
- Fighter machine gun weapons are replaced with anti-air missiles.
- Shotgun weapons are replaced with lightning weapons. They have a longer burst duration.
- Burst-fire plasma weapons are replaced with single-shot plasma.
- Impact-cluster plasma weapons are replaced with single-shot plasma. They look good as hell.
- Each napalm weapon received a unique replacement weapon system suited to its role. I bet you thought all napalm units were the same. Sad.
- The guidance laser weapon of the Medusa is replaced by a red laser weapon. Its missiles do not retarget.
- The mini-Juno missiles of the Blindfold are replaced by EMP bombs.

Legion-specific mechanics are removed or replaced:

- Drone spawners are either removed or their drones are replaced with the Noctua.
- Reactive armor is removed and converted into some unit health.
- The Martyr, Phoenix, Octeres, and Astraeus are removed.
- The high trajectory smart-weapon of the Satyr is removed.
- The Cacophony and Gelasma have higher range and are not transportable (to act as HLT variants).
- The Triton and the Scylla are no longer amphibious.
- The heatray and drone destroyer (Syracusia) is replaced by a conventional destroyer.
- The torpedo frigate (Argonaut) cannot target underwater units. [This needs work; you can aim at underwater mexes manually.]
- Air scouts and bombers are copied over to the Legion air lab.
- The linear minelayer bomber is converted to a conventional bomber.
- The upgraded advanced geothermal has no radar jammer and no drones. It is cheaper.
